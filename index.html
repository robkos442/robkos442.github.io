Sandhills Recovery is an online community built for anyone in any stage of recovery.

The goal of this website is to provide factual information and helpful resources to anyone that is looking to make a change. We fact-check all of the information that we display and will provide sources for any information that is posted. You will find this site helpful whether you are thinking about changing your drinking habits, have years of sobriety, or you are somewhere in between.

I'm Rob, I struggle with alcoholism and I have for years. I have learned a lot on this path to recovery and I'm still learning every day. When I was looking for help online, I noticed that I had so many questions that I couldn't find answers to. I want to provide as much information as I can for others that are in similar situations as I am. If you notice that anything on this site needs to be adjusted or if you have any questions that you would like addressed, please, feel free to contact me any time

I feel that information like this should be available anonymously and at no cost. I will never use any means of tracking on this website. You will never have to "sign up" for anything. There will never be any features hidden behind a paywall or a "pro" subscription. If you find this website to be helpful, you can use the links provided to purchase any books and I will receive a small amount from each transaction to help cover the cost of hosting fees.

At the time of this writing, I am the only person that is building and maintaining this site. If you would like to contribute by writing articles, doing research, helping with development, etc... please feel free to contact me. Your help will be appreciated by myself and anyone that finds this website helpful.

Again, the more that people reach out and provide feedback, the better we can help each other out. You can get ahold of me directly by using the green contact button on the bottom right corner of the page. You can also use the anonymous chat application to communicate with others.

Thank you for reading,
I will not drink with you today.
-Rob
